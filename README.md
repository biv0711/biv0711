## What I am building

I am the founder and CEO of Rhemic and Mebbian.

Rhemic is the AI-native agency I am bootstrapping toward $100k MRR. Mebbian is the infrastructure company coming out of that operating reality: the substrate underneath business agents.

The bet is simple: agents are only useful when they know the business. Not just docs. Not just chat history. The real operating memory: customers, workflows, decisions, tradeoffs, context, and why things changed.

Current focus:

- Per-business memory substrate for onboarding AI agents.
- Knowledge graphs and context graphs with decision provenance.
- Agent packets that ride on the substrate, starting with after-hours response.
- AI-native agency systems that turn service work into repeatable software.
- Dogfooding the system inside my own company before selling it outside.
